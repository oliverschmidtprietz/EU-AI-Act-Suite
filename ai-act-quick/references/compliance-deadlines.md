# AI Act Compliance Deadlines — Quick Reference

Condensed timeline for triage urgency calculation. Just the 4 phase dates, scope, and transition rules.

**Last verified:** March 2026.

---

## 4 Regulatory Phases

> **AI Omnibus 2026 postponement applied.** Phase 3 (Annex III) and Phase 4 (Annex I) dates reflect the AI Omnibus postponement (Annex III: 2 Aug 2026 → 2 Dec 2027; Annex I: 2 Aug 2027 → 2 Aug 2028). See `../../ai-act-high-risk/references/ai-omnibus-timeline-postponements.md`.

| Phase | Date | Status | Scope | Key Penalty |
|-------|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| **1** | **2 Feb 2025** | **IN EFFECT** | Prohibited practices (Art. 5) + AI literacy (Art. 4) | EUR 35M / 7% turnover |
| **2** | **2 Aug 2025** | **IN EFFECT** | GPAI obligations (Art. 53, 55) + governance bodies | EUR 15M / 3% turnover |
| **3** | **2 Dec 2027** | **UPCOMING** *(Omnibus-postponed from 2 Aug 2026)* | High-risk Annex III + Art. 50 transparency + deployer obligations | EUR 15M / 3% turnover |
| **4** | **2 Aug 2028** | **Future** *(Omnibus-postponed from 2 Aug 2027)* | High-risk Annex I (product safety AI) | EUR 15M / 3% turnover |

## Transition Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Existing systems** | Pre-Dec 2027 systems *(Omnibus-postponed from pre-Aug 2026)*: obligations apply only on "significant change" in design |
| **EU large-scale IT** | SIS II, VIS, Eurodac etc.: extended to **31 Dec 2030** |
| **Pre-Aug 2025 GPAI** | 2-year grace period → comply by **2 Aug 2027** |
