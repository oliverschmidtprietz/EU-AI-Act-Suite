# AI Act Compliance Deadlines

Extracted from the master compliance timeline. Covers the 4 regulatory phases and transition rules relevant to role-based obligations.

**Last verified:** March 2026. Always search for latest regulatory milestones.

---

## 1. Master Timeline — 4 Regulatory Phases

### Phase 1: 2 February 2025 — IN EFFECT

**Scope:** Prohibited practices (Art. 5) + AI literacy (Art. 4)

| Obligation | Article | Who | Status |
|-----------|---------|-----|--------|
| All prohibited AI practices banned | Art. 5 | All actors | **Enforceable now** |
| AI competence measures in place | Art. 4 | All actors (all risk tiers) | **Enforceable now** |

**Penalty for Art. 5 violation:** Up to EUR 35M or 7% global turnover (Art. 99(3))

### Phase 2: 2 August 2025 — IN EFFECT

**Scope:** GPAI model obligations + Governance bodies operational

| Obligation | Article | Who | Status |
|-----------|---------|-----|--------|
| GPAI model transparency obligations | Art. 53 | GPAI model providers | **Enforceable now** |
| GPAI systemic risk obligations | Art. 55 | Systemic risk GPAI providers | **Enforceable now** |
| AI Office fully operational | Art. 64 | EU Commission | **Operational** |
| AI Board operational | Art. 65 | Member States | **Operational** |
| Codes of Practice for GPAI | Art. 56 | GPAI providers (voluntary) | **Final CoP endorsed Aug 2025** |
| Penalty provisions applicable | Art. 99-101 | All actors | **Enforceable** |
| Confidentiality and information exchange rules | Art. 70, 78 | Authorities | **Active** |

### Phase 3: 2 August 2026 — UPCOMING

**Scope:** High-risk AI systems listed in Annex III + Most other AI Act provisions

| Obligation | Article | Who | Deadline |
|-----------|---------|-----|----------|
| Full high-risk provider obligations | Art. 8-17 | Providers of Annex III systems | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Deployer obligations for high-risk | Art. 26 | Deployers of Annex III systems | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Art. 50 transparency obligations | Art. 50 | All relevant actors | 2 Aug 2026 |
| EU database registration | Art. 49 | Providers + deployers of high-risk | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Market surveillance system operational | Art. 74-82 | National authorities | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Post-market monitoring | Art. 72 | Providers of high-risk | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Serious incident reporting | Art. 73 | Providers of high-risk | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Conformity assessment procedures | Art. 43 | Providers (certain categories) | 2 Aug 2026 |
| AI regulatory sandboxes established | Art. 57 | Member States (at least 1 per MS) | 2 Aug 2026 |
| FRIA for certain deployers | Art. 27 | Public body deployers + specific private | 2 Aug 2026 |
| Importer and distributor obligations | Art. 23-24 | Importers, distributors | 2 Aug 2026 |

### Phase 4: 2 August 2027

**Scope:** High-risk AI systems embedded in products (Annex I product safety legislation)

| Obligation | Article | Who | Deadline |
|-----------|---------|-----|----------|
| High-risk obligations for Annex I systems | Art. 6(1) + Art. 8-17 | Providers of AI in products | 2 Aug 2027 |
| Conformity assessment integration with product safety | Art. 43 | Providers + notified bodies | 2 Aug 2027 |
| Product safety legislation alignment | Annex I entries | Product manufacturers | 2 Aug 2027 |

### Special Transition Rules

| Rule | Article | Detail |
|------|---------|--------|
| **Existing high-risk systems** | Art. 111(2) | Systems already on market before 2 Aug 2026: obligations apply only if system undergoes "significant change" in design after that date |
| **Large-scale IT systems (EU)** | Art. 111(3) | EU large-scale IT systems (SIS II, VIS, Eurodac, etc.): compliance deadline extended to **2 Aug 2030** |
| **GPAI models on market before Aug 2025** | Art. 111(3a) | GPAI model providers must comply by 2 Aug 2027 (2-year grace period) |
