## Full Title

COMMISSION IMPLEMENTING DECISION on a standardisation request to the European Committee for Standardisation and the European Committee for Electrotechnical Standardisation as regards high-risk AI systems in support of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 of the European Parliament and of the Council and repealing Implementing Decision C(2023)3215.

## Scope

This implementing decision establishes a standardisation request directed at CEN and CENELEC to develop harmonised standards for high-risk AI systems under the EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689). It replaces the earlier Implementing Decision C(2023)3215.

The standardisation request covers **10 key areas**:

1. Risk management
2. Governance and quality of datasets
3. Record keeping
4. Transparency
5. Human oversight
6. Accuracy
7. Robustness
8. Cybersecurity
9. Quality management
10. Conformity assessment
