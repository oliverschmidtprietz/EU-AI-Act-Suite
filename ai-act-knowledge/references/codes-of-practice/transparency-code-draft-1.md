## Publication Details

Published 17 December 2025 as a policy and legislation document.

## Scope and Purpose

This first draft of the Code addresses key considerations for providers and deployers of AI systems generating content falling within the scope of Article 50(2) and (4) of the AI Act.

This draft of the Code of Practice constitutes a foundation for further refinement. It is the result of a collaborative effort involving hundreds of participants from industry, academia, and civil society, as well as contributions from Member States.
