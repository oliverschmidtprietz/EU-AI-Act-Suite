## Overview

This study supports the Impact Assessment of the European Commission's proposed Regulation on a European approach to Artificial Intelligence (AI). It was published alongside the AI Act proposal on 21 April 2021.

The study provides the independent evidence base -- including market analysis, cost estimates, and stakeholder consultations -- that informed the Commission's impact assessment of the proposed regulation.
