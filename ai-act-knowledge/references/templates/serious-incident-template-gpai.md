## Purpose and Context

Under Article 55 of the EU AI Act, providers of general-purpose AI models with systemic risk are required to report relevant information on serious incidents to the AI Office and, where appropriate, to national competent authorities.

The publication of this template promotes consistent and transparent reporting and helps providers demonstrate compliance with the commitments set out in **Commitment 9 of the GPAI Code of Practice**.

Published 4 November 2025.

## Legal Basis

### Article 55 — Obligations for Providers of GPAI Models with Systemic Risk

Providers must report relevant information on serious incidents to the AI Office and, where appropriate, to national competent authorities.

### GPAI Code of Practice — Commitment 9

The Code of Practice for general-purpose AI further operationalises this obligation by specifying the information to be included in such reports as part of demonstrating compliance.

### Commission Guidelines for GPAI Providers

The Commission's [Guidelines for providers of general-purpose AI models](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/guidelines-gpai-providers) help developers understand if they need to comply with the obligations under the AI Act.
