| 7 | Distributor | Haendler | Makes AI system available (not provider/importer) |
| 12 | Intended purpose | Zweckbestimmung | Purpose documented by provider |
| 23 | Substantial modification | Wesentliche Veraenderung | Unforeseen change affecting compliance |
