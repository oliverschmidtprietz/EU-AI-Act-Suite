# Annex I Section A vs Section B mapping

## Why this matters

- **Section A (NLF-based)** → full Chapter III applies (Art. 8–17 quality/risk/data/transparency/oversight/accuracy etc.).
- **Section B (other Union harmonisation legislation)** → only Art. 6(1), 102–109, 112 apply per Art. 2(2) AI Act.

## Worked classification consequences

- An AI safety component of an MDR-regulated medical device → Section A → full Chapter III applies, integrated with MDR conformity assessment per Art. 8(2).
- An AI safety component of an automotive system under Reg. (EU) 2018/858 → Section B → only Art. 6(1), 102–109, 112 apply.
