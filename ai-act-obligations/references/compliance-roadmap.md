# AI Act Compliance Roadmap

Extracted from the master compliance timeline. Covers the regulatory phases, quarterly action calendar, resource estimation, and phased roadmap template for obligation implementation planning.

**Last verified:** March 2026. Always search for latest regulatory milestones.

---

## 1. Master Timeline — 4 Regulatory Phases

### Phase 1: 2 February 2025 — IN EFFECT

**Scope:** Prohibited practices (Art. 5) + AI literacy (Art. 4)

| Obligation | Article | Who | Status |
|-----------|---------|-----|--------|
| All prohibited AI practices banned | Art. 5 | All actors | **Enforceable now** |
| AI competence measures in place | Art. 4 | All actors (all risk tiers) | **Enforceable now** |

**Penalty for Art. 5 violation:** Up to EUR 35M or 7% global turnover (Art. 99(3))

### Phase 2: 2 August 2025 — IN EFFECT

**Scope:** GPAI model obligations + Governance bodies operational

| Obligation | Article | Who | Status |
|-----------|---------|-----|--------|
| GPAI model transparency obligations | Art. 53 | GPAI model providers | **Enforceable now** |
| GPAI systemic risk obligations | Art. 55 | Systemic risk GPAI providers | **Enforceable now** |
| AI Office fully operational | Art. 64 | EU Commission | **Operational** |
| AI Board operational | Art. 65 | Member States | **Operational** |
| Codes of Practice for GPAI | Art. 56 | GPAI providers (voluntary) | **Final CoP endorsed Aug 2025** |
| Penalty provisions applicable | Art. 99-101 | All actors | **Enforceable** |
| Confidentiality and information exchange rules | Art. 70, 78 | Authorities | **Active** |

> **AI Omnibus 2026 postponement applied.** The original Art. 113 dates (2 Aug 2026 for Annex III; 2 Aug 2027 for Annex I) have been postponed by the AI Omnibus to **2 December 2027** and **2 August 2028** respectively. The dates in Phase 3 and Phase 4 below reflect the post-Omnibus operative timeline. See `../../ai-act-high-risk/references/ai-omnibus-timeline-postponements.md` for the citation chain.

### Phase 3: 2 December 2027 — UPCOMING (Omnibus-postponed from 2 Aug 2026)

**Scope:** High-risk AI systems listed in Annex III + Most other AI Act provisions

| Obligation | Article | Who | Deadline |
|-----------|---------|-----|----------|
| Full high-risk provider obligations | Art. 8-17 | Providers of Annex III systems | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Deployer obligations for high-risk | Art. 26 | Deployers of Annex III systems | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Art. 50 transparency obligations | Art. 50 | All relevant actors | 2 Dec 2027 |
| EU database registration | Art. 49 | Providers + deployers of high-risk | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Market surveillance system operational | Art. 74-82 | National authorities | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Post-market monitoring | Art. 72 | Providers of high-risk | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Serious incident reporting | Art. 73 | Providers of high-risk | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Conformity assessment procedures | Art. 43 | Providers (certain categories) | 2 Dec 2027 |
| AI regulatory sandboxes established | Art. 57 | Member States (at least 1 per MS) | 2 Aug 2026 (unchanged) |
| FRIA for certain deployers | Art. 27 | Public body deployers + specific private | 2 Dec 2027 |
| Importer and distributor obligations | Art. 23-24 | Importers, distributors | 2 Dec 2027 |

### Phase 4: 2 August 2028 (Omnibus-postponed from 2 Aug 2027)

**Scope:** High-risk AI systems embedded in products (Annex I product safety legislation)

| Obligation | Article | Who | Deadline |
|-----------|---------|-----|----------|
| High-risk obligations for Annex I systems | Art. 6(1) + Art. 8-17 | Providers of AI in products | 2 Aug 2028 |
| Conformity assessment integration with product safety | Art. 43 | Providers + notified bodies | 2 Aug 2028 |
| Product safety legislation alignment | Annex I entries | Product manufacturers | 2 Aug 2028 |

### Special Transition Rules

| Rule | Article | Detail |
|------|---------|--------|
| **Existing high-risk systems** | Art. 111(2) | Systems already on market before 2 Dec 2027 (Omnibus-postponed from 2 Aug 2026): obligations apply only if system undergoes "significant change" in design after that date |
| **Large-scale IT systems (EU)** | Art. 111(3) | EU large-scale IT systems (SIS II, VIS, Eurodac, etc.): compliance deadline extended to **2 Aug 2030** |
| **GPAI models on market before Aug 2025** | Art. 111(3a) | GPAI model providers must comply by 2 Aug 2027 (2-year grace period) |

## 5. Phased Compliance Roadmap Template

### Phase A: Awareness & Governance Foundation (Months 1-2)

**Objective:** Establish organizational awareness and governance structure

| Deliverable | Responsible | Acceptance Criteria |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| AI governance steering committee established | Executive management | Named members, terms of reference, meeting cadence |
| AI system inventory initiated | IT + Business units | Comprehensive questionnaire distributed; collection underway |
| AI competence baseline assessed (Art. 4) | HR + Legal | Gap analysis complete; training plan drafted |
| Legal counsel with AI Act expertise engaged | Legal | Engagement letter; initial briefing complete |
| Budget and resource allocation approved | Executive management | Approved project budget and timeline |
| National regulatory requirements mapped | Legal | Per-jurisdiction obligation register created |

### Phase B: Inventory & Classification (Months 2-4)

**Objective:** Complete AI system inventory and risk classification

| Deliverable | Responsible | Acceptance Criteria |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| AI system inventory complete | IT + Business units | All AI systems catalogued with metadata |
| Per-system classification completed | Legal + Technical | Classification per /ai-act-classifier methodology |
| Role determination per system completed | Legal | Role per /ai-act-roles methodology |
| Prohibited practice screening passed | Legal | All systems screened; no Art. 5 violations |
| Classification documentation archived | Legal + Compliance | Formal classification records filed |
| GPAI model identification (if applicable) | Technical | All GPAI models identified and classified |

### Phase C: Gap Analysis & Planning (Months 3-5)

**Objective:** Identify compliance gaps and create detailed implementation plan

| Deliverable | Responsible | Acceptance Criteria |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Per-system obligation mapping completed | Legal + Compliance | Obligations matrix per /ai-act-obligations methodology |
| Current compliance status assessed | All stakeholders | Per-obligation gap rating (in place / partial / not addressed) |
| Implementation plan created | Project management | Per-gap action items with owner, deadline, effort |
| Resource plan finalized | Executive management | Resources allocated per implementation plan |
| Works council engagement initiated (DE/AT/NL) | HR + Legal | Formal notification; negotiation timeline |
| DPIA/FRIA requirements identified | DPO + Legal | Assessment scope and methodology agreed |

### Phase D: Implementation (Months 4-10)

**Objective:** Implement all required compliance measures

| Deliverable | Responsible | Acceptance Criteria |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Risk management system operational (Art. 9) | Risk management | Documented system; initial risk assessment complete |
| Quality management system operational (Art. 17) | Quality management | QMS documented; processes active |
| Technical documentation complete (Art. 11) | Technical + Legal | Full documentation set per Art. 11 requirements |
| Data governance framework operational (Art. 10) | Data management | Data quality measures; bias testing; documentation |
| Human oversight measures implemented (Art. 14) | Operations + IT | Oversight procedures; trained personnel |
| Transparency measures active (Art. 13, 50) | Operations + Comms | User-facing information; AI disclosure measures |
| Logging infrastructure operational (Art. 12, 26(6)) | IT | Auto-generated logs captured; 6-month retention |
| Incident reporting procedure established (Art. 73) | Legal + IT | Procedure documented; reporting channels tested |
| DPIA completed (Art. 26(9)) | DPO + Legal | DPIA report approved; measures implemented |
| FRIA completed (Art. 27, if required) | Legal + HR | FRIA report approved |
| Works council agreement signed (if applicable) | HR + Legal | Betriebsvereinbarung / works council consent |
| AI competence training delivered (Art. 4) | HR | Training completed; records maintained |
| Post-market monitoring system operational (Art. 72) | Quality + IT | Monitoring plan active; data collection |

### Phase E: Verification & Registration (Months 9-12)

**Objective:** Verify compliance, complete conformity assessment, register

| Deliverable | Responsible | Acceptance Criteria |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Internal compliance audit completed | Compliance / Internal audit | Audit report; all critical findings resolved |
| Conformity assessment completed (Art. 43) | Provider + Notified body (if needed) | Declaration of conformity issued |
| CE marking affixed (if applicable) | Provider | CE marking on system/documentation |
| EU database registration completed (Art. 49) | Legal + IT | Registration confirmed in EU database |
| Compliance file archived | Legal + Compliance | Complete compliance file accessible |
| Deployer registration completed (Art. 49(3)) | Deployer | Deployment registered |

### Phase F: Ongoing Compliance (Continuous)

**Objective:** Maintain compliance through continuous monitoring and improvement

| Activity | Frequency | Responsible |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| System performance monitoring | Continuous | IT + Operations |
| Risk management review | Annually (minimum) | Risk management |
| Documentation updates | As changes occur | Technical + Legal |
| AI competence training refresh | Annually | HR |
| DPIA review and update | Annually or on significant change | DPO |
| Post-market monitoring review | Per monitoring plan | Quality |
| Incident reporting and analysis | As incidents occur | Legal + IT |
| Regulatory update monitoring | Quarterly | Legal + Compliance |
| Works council information update | As changes occur (DE/AT/NL) | HR + Legal |
| Internal audit | Annually | Internal audit |
| Authority cooperation | As requested | Legal |
